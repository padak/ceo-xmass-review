# CEO Assessment Questions

> User is identified automatically via Keboola OIDC (X-Kbc-User-Email header)

1. What have you shipped in the last 3 weeks that made our customers' lives better?

2. In which areas do you support me so well that I don't have to think about them?

3. What do you excel at?

4. What do your colleagues excel at that you admire or learn from?

5. What bottlenecks with other departments, if cleared, would help you most?

6. If you could magically eliminate 3 activities that cause you the most pain, which would they be?

7. Self-evaluation:
   a) What are you struggling with? Where could I (CEO) help you improve?
   b) What are you great at that might be underutilized and could help the company more?

8. What would you do if you were in my (CEO) role?
   a) Next 7 days?
   b) Next 30 days?
   c) Next 90 days?

9. Pick any role(s) in the company - what would you do differently if you were in that role?
   (You can comment on multiple roles)

10. What are our top 3 priorities and why? What outcomes will they bring to customers and revenue?

11. What is THE single most important priority for the foreseeable future? The one thing that would have 50%+ impact?
